# Brand Voice Calibration Framework

## The 4 Voice Axes

Every brand voice can be plotted on these 4 axes:

### 1. Formality (Casual <-> Formal)
| Level | Vocabulary | Sentences | Examples |
|-------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Very Casual | Slang, contractions, emoji | Short, fragments OK | "Hey! Check this out" |
| Casual | Contractions, conversational | Short-medium | "We've got something for you" |
| Neutral | Standard business English | Medium | "We're pleased to share" |
| Formal | No contractions, precise | Longer, complete | "We are delighted to present" |
| Very Formal | Technical/legal vocabulary | Complex, structured | "We hereby announce" |

### 2. Energy (Calm <-> Energetic)
| Level | Tone | Punctuation | Examples |
|-------|------|-------------|----------|
| Calm | Measured, reassuring | Periods | "Take your time exploring." |
| Moderate | Warm, encouraging | Periods, occasional ! | "You'll love what's inside." |
| Energetic | Excited, motivating | Exclamation marks | "This changes everything!" |

### 3. Personality (Serious <-> Playful)
| Level | Style | Examples |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Serious | Facts, data, authority | "Our research shows a 23% improvement" |
| Balanced | Facts with personality | "Here's the good news: 23% better results" |
| Playful | Humor, wordplay, creativity | "Your emails just got 23% more awesome" |

### 4. Perspective (Brand-Centric <-> Reader-Centric)
| Focus | Pronoun | Examples |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Brand | "We", "Our" | "We created this for you" |
| Balanced | Mix | "We built it, you'll love it" |
| Reader | "You", "Your" | "Your new favorite tool is here" |

## Voice Mapping Process

When brand voice guidelines are provided:
1. Plot the brand on each of the 4 axes
2. Select vocabulary tier matching formality level
3. Match sentence length and structure to energy level
4. Apply personality level to word choice and metaphors
5. Set pronoun balance based on perspective preference

## Vocabulary Transformation Examples

### "We're launching a new product"
| Voice | Transformation |
|-------|---------------|
| Formal + Calm | "We are pleased to announce the introduction of our latest offering" |
| Casual + Energetic | "Big news — we just dropped something amazing!" |
| Formal + Energetic | "We are thrilled to unveil our groundbreaking new solution" |
| Casual + Calm | "Hey, we've got something new to show you" |
| Playful + Reader-centric | "Your inbox just got an upgrade (you're welcome)" |

## Common Brand Voice Archetypes

### The Authority
Formal, calm, serious, brand-centric
- Financial services, legal, healthcare
- "Based on our analysis, we recommend..."

### The Friend
Casual, moderate energy, balanced, reader-centric
- DTC brands, lifestyle, food/beverage
- "You're going to love this..."

### The Innovator
Neutral-formal, energetic, balanced, brand-centric
- Tech companies, SaaS, startups
- "Introducing the future of..."

### The Coach
Casual, energetic, balanced, reader-centric
- Fitness, education, personal development
- "Ready to level up? Let's go!"

### The Storyteller
Casual-neutral, calm, playful, reader-centric
- Luxury, travel, experiences
- "Imagine waking up to this view..."
